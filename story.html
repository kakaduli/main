THE BIRTH OF YURRAH

Yurrah Skin care range was formulated by a group of scientists with more than a decade of research in the field of indigenous plant and herbal applications.

Yurrah skin care products were tested on willing family members to obtain research and feedback on ways to improve and further develop our formula. Through the process we have perfected our formula to suit multiple skin types and treat different skin conditions.

Australian plant life is forever growing and changing. Due to this our specialised team of scientist are constantly making improvements to our products making them as effective as possible with all natural raw ingredients.

Our Kakadu formula is derived from the Kakadu plum. We have chosen Kakadu plum extract as the base of our formula due to extensive research showing that the people who migrated to Kakadu, Australia 40000 years ago had very healthy skin despite excessive sun exposure.

This was due to consuming of Kakadu plum. Kakadu plum has up to 100 times of Vitamin C of oranges and over 100 beneficial ingredients such as Vitamin E and polyphenols proving it to be the perfect base ingredient for our formula. We also use other natural ingredients such as tasmannia Lanceolata fruit extract and tea tree oil.
